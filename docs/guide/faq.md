# FAQ

> Part of the [Q‑Art User Guide](./README.md).

## About the method

**Q‑Art told me nothing I didn't already know. Is it working?**
Probably — that's what working feels like. Q‑Art never adds information; it re-arranges what *you* put in until the pattern is visible. "I already knew that" usually means: you knew every piece, and had never seen them side by side.

**Is this therapy? Coaching?**
No. Q‑Art borrows questioning techniques from established traditions, but it is a *thinking tool* — not therapy, and not medical, legal, or financial advice. If you're in distress, contact your local emergency number (112 EU · 911 US · 999 UK) or a crisis line. This notice is always one tap away on the **About** page.

**Why does it never give me an answer?**
By design. Advice from outside rarely survives contact with your real constraints. A question *you* reformulated does — because the answer inside it is already yours. If you want a recommendation engine, Q‑Art will frustrate you; if you want to get unstuck, that's the mechanism.

**What if the reading feels wrong?**
Take what's useful, leave the rest — the reading says so itself. It's deterministic: it only mirrors what you ticked and wrote, so a thin map gives a thin reading. Tick a few more facets (especially the ones flagged under *"Where you haven't looked yet"*) and it sharpens.

**Do I have to fill everything?**
No. Ticking is enough; every facet is optional; weighting is an optional refinement, not a grade. Honest beats complete.

**What's a "cycle"? Why start a second one?**
One cycle = one pass over one question. Its deliverable is the reformulated question. The method's key move is **recursion**: run a fresh pass *on that new question*. The second pass is usually shallower, faster — and it's where the action plan gets built. Stop when a pass stops surfacing anything new.

## About the app

**Does it work offline?**
Yes. Once loaded, the app shell is cached and everything runs locally. You can install it from your browser like an app (Add to Home Screen / Install).

**Where exactly is my data? Who can read it?**
On this device only, encrypted at rest. No account, no server, no analytics. Details: [Your data](./your-data.md).

**Can I use it on my phone and my laptop?**
There's no sync in v1 (that would need a server). Move a session by **exporting** the dossier on one device and **importing** it on the other.

**I refreshed / closed the tab — is my work gone?**
No. Everything autosaves, encrypted. The landing page offers to continue, and opening a GUI directly resumes your latest session.

**What are the three weighting methods?**
Optional ways to say what matters most, under *"Weigh what matters"* in the synthesis: **Steppers** (direct 1–5), **Most/least** (pick the extremes in small sets), **Marbles** (spend 10 across your items). All keyboard-friendly, none required. Use whichever feels natural — your choice is remembered.

**English or French?**
Both, fully — including every proposition in the question banks. Toggle EN/FR in the header at any time; even the synthesis re-renders in the new language.

**Something is broken. How do I report it without exposing my content?**
About → **Download diagnostics**: a content-free bundle (codes, counts, timings — never your words) that's safe to share with a bug report. Security contact: see `SECURITY.md`.
